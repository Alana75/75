6 How to Submit a Request for Access to Records

To submit a formal request for access to records held by Ralph Analytics, follow these steps:

  1. Complete the prescribed form: Use Form C as prescribed by the Promotion of Access to Information Act Regulations (available from the Information Regulator website)
  2. Submit the form to our Information Officer at: privacy@ralph-analytics.com
  3. Pay the prescribed fee: A request fee of R50 is payable on submission (waived for indigent requestors). An access fee may apply depending on the form in which records are provided.
  4. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request, as required by PAIA Section 25.

Request address: privacy@ralph-analytics.com
Subject line: "PAIA Access Request — [Your Name]"
Response time: 30 days (may be extended by 30 days in exceptional circumstances per PAIA S26)

7 Grounds for Refusal

Access to records may be refused on the grounds set out in PAIA Chapter 4, including but not limited to:

  • Protection of third-party personal information (PAIA S63)
  • Protection of commercial information (PAIA S64)
  • Protection of confidential information (PAIA S65)
  • Safety of individuals and property (PAIA S66)
  • Legally privileged information (PAIA S67)
  • Records that cannot be found or do not exist (PAIA S23)

8 Remedies Available When a Request is Refused

If your request for access to records is refused, or you are not satisfied with a decision, you may:

  • Apply to the Information Regulator for a review of the decision (PAIA S89A)
  • Apply to the High Court for relief (PAIA S82)
