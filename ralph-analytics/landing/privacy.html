Privacy Policy

How Ralph Analytics collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective date: 26 June 2026 Last updated: 26 June 2026 Jurisdiction: Republic of South Africa Framework: POPIA · GDPR (where applicable) · EU AI Act
✓ POPIA S18 Compliant — Information Notice

1 Who We Are

Ralph Analytics (Pty) Ltd ("Ralph Analytics", "we", "us", "our") is a South African company providing Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) analytics, regulatory compliance management, and ESG intelligence services to enterprise clients.

Registered Address: South Africa
Email: admin@ralph-analytics.com
Website: https://ralph-analytics.com
Information Officer: To be registered with the Information Regulator (IRCO) in accordance with POPIA Section 55.

For all privacy-related enquiries, contact us at: privacy@ralph-analytics.com

2 Personal Information We Collect

We collect personal information only where necessary to provide our services. The categories of personal information we process include:

  • Identity data: Full name, job title, organisation name
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number
  • Account data: Login credentials (passwords are bcrypt-hashed and never stored in plaintext), account preferences
  • Usage data: Platform activity logs, feature usage, access timestamps
  • Compliance data: Documents, audit records, and compliance information submitted by clients on behalf of their organisations
  • Communication data: Support enquiries and messages submitted via our contact form

We do not collect special categories of personal information (race, health, biometric data, etc.) in the ordinary course of our business.

3 How We Use Your Personal Information

We process personal information for the following purposes, each with a documented lawful basis under POPIA Section 11:

  • Service delivery (Contractual basis): Providing the platform, managing accounts, processing compliance data
  • Authentication (Contractual basis): Verifying identity and maintaining secure access to the platform
  • Communication (Legitimate interest): Responding to enquiries, sending service notifications and support responses
  • Platform improvement (Legitimate interest): Analysing usage patterns to improve features and performance
  • Legal compliance (Legal obligation): Meeting obligations under POPIA, tax law, and other applicable legislation
  • Security (Legitimate interest): Detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorised access, and system abuse

4 Artificial Intelligence — Transparency & Governance

⚡ EU AI Act Art. 13 & 22 · POPIA S11 & S71 · SA Draft National AI Policy 2026

Ralph Analytics uses artificial intelligence (AI) tools to assist in the delivery of compliance analytics, audit readiness, and M&E reporting services. We are committed to full transparency about how AI is used, governed, and safeguarded.

4.1 AI Model & Governance Framework

  • AI model used: OpenAI GPT-4o (via OpenAI API only — not via ChatGPT.com or consumer interfaces)
  • AI governance policy: All AI use is governed by our Internal AI Governance & Usage Policy (RA-POL-AI-001 v1.1), aligned with the EU AI Act, POPIA, GDPR, NIST AI RMF, and ISO/IEC 42001
  • Risk classification: Our AI use cases are classified per the EU AI Act risk framework (Minimal, Limited, High Risk). Prohibited practices are strictly excluded.
  • EU AI Act compliance: As the EU AI Act applies in full from 2 August 2026, Ralph Analytics has conducted a formal compliance review. A signed Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA-AI-001) is on file.

4.2 What AI Is Used For

  • Compliance gap analysis — reviewing client documents against regulatory requirements
  • Audit narrative generation — drafting findings summaries and corrective action recommendations
  • Risk scoring and classification — categorising identified risks by severity and framework alignment
  • Report summarisation — condensing audit data into executive outputs
  • Standards interpretation — mapping client practices against ISO, ESG, SLCP, and M&E frameworks

4.3 Human Oversight & Your Rights (POPIA S71 / GDPR Art. 22)

  • No fully automated decisions: Ralph Analytics does not make fully automated decisions about individuals that produce legal or similarly significant effects. All AI outputs require human review before use.
  • Human validation: Every AI-generated report, analysis, or recommendation is reviewed and validated by a qualified Ralph Analytics professional before delivery to clients.
  • Right to explanation: You may request an explanation of how AI was used in any analysis or deliverable. Contact us at privacy@ralph-analytics.com.
  • Right to object: You may object to AI-assisted processing of your personal data. We will accommodate reasonable objections where operationally feasible.

4.4 Data Protection in AI Processing

  • PII stripping: We use a technical SanitiseForAI process to remove personally identifiable information from inputs before they are submitted to the AI model.
  • No training on client data: Under our signed OpenAI Enterprise Data Processing Agreement (DPA), OpenAI does not use data submitted via our API to train or improve its models.
  • Data minimisation: We submit only the minimum data necessary to perform the requested analysis.
  • Cross-border transfer safeguards: OpenAI processes data in the United States. This transfer is governed by EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) under our signed DPA, satisfying POPIA Section 72 and GDPR Article 46. A Transfer Impact Assessment (TIA) is on file.

4.5 AI Disclosure in Deliverables

⚡ AI-assisted content label: All reports and analyses produced with AI assistance include the following disclosure statement: "AI-assisted analysis (OpenAI GPT-4o) — reviewed and validated by Ralph Analytics. Advisory only — independently verify material facts before relying on this output."

4.6 Information Officer & AI Governance Contact

  • Information Officer (POPIA): Registered with the Information Regulator of South Africa in accordance with POPIA Section 55
  • Contact: privacy@ralph-analytics.com | admin@ralph-analytics.com
  • AI governance queries: For questions about how AI was used in your engagement, contact your account manager or email privacy@ralph-analytics.com

5 Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share personal information with:

  • OpenAI (AI processor): Document content for AI analysis. Governed by a Data Processing Agreement. OpenAI does not use this data to train models under our enterprise terms.
  • Hosting provider (Cloudways/DigitalOcean): Infrastructure provider hosting our servers. Governed by their data processing terms.
  • Email service providers: For transactional emails (password resets, notifications). Governed by processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities: Where required by law, court order, or regulatory obligation.

6 International Transfers (POPIA Section 72)

Some of our service providers are located outside South Africa, including OpenAI (United States). When we transfer personal information outside South Africa, we ensure that:

  • The recipient country provides adequate data protection, or
  • We have entered into binding contractual arrangements (Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent) ensuring equivalent protection, or
  • The transfer is necessary for the performance of our contract with you

OpenAI processes data under the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, which provide equivalent protection to POPIA requirements.

7 Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law:

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account, plus 3 years after closure
  • Compliance records: Retained for 5 years (aligned with typical audit and regulatory requirements)
  • Communication logs: Retained for 2 years
  • AI-generated outputs: Retained for the duration of the client engagement

8 Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information in accordance with POPIA Section 19, including:

  • All passwords are bcrypt-hashed (minimum 10 salt rounds)
  • All platform access requires JWT-authenticated sessions with automatic expiry
  • HTTPS enforced across all endpoints via Cloudflare SSL
  • DDoS protection and Web Application Firewall (WAF) via Cloudflare
  • Server access restricted to SSH key-based authentication only
  • Regular security reviews and penetration testing

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify the Information Regulator and affected individuals within the timeframes required by POPIA.

9 Your Rights (POPIA Sections 23–25)

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal information on grounds of legitimate interest
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@ralph-analytics.com with the subject line "Data Subject Request". We will respond within 30 days.

10 Cookies and Tracking

Ralph Analytics uses minimal cookies for essential platform functionality only:

  • Authentication tokens: Stored in localStorage to maintain your session
  • We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or analytics cookies
  • No third-party tracking scripts are embedded in the platform
