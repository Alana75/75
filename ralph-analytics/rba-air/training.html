Maturity Progression Roadmap
Key milestones to advance from Level 1 → Level 5
Level 1 → 2: Establish AI Ethics Policy · Commission AI inventory · Assign AI governance accountability · Basic staff awareness
Level 2 → 3: Complete EU AI Act risk classification · Formalise AI governance committee · Deploy mandatory training programme · Implement incident reporting
Level 3 → 4: Implement AIAs for all high-risk systems · Full supplier AI declarations · Automated monitoring dashboards · Quarterly KPI reviews
Level 4 → 5: Predictive AI risk models · Third-party certification (ISO 42001) · Continuous improvement loop · Sector benchmarking
