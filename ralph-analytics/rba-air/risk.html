EU AI Act Risk Tier Reference
EU AI Act Title II & III · AIR-2.2
⛔ Unacceptable Risk
PROHIBITED. Halt and notify compliance immediately if identified.

Examples: social scoring · real-time biometric surveillance in public · subliminal manipulation
⚠️ High Risk
Mandatory controls required. AIA required before deployment.

Examples: biometric ID · employment AI · critical infrastructure · law enforcement · education AI
ℹ️ Limited Risk
Transparency obligations only. Disclosure required to users.

Examples: chatbots · emotion recognition · synthetic content · biometric categorisation
✅ Minimal Risk
Voluntary guidelines apply. No mandatory controls.

Examples: spam filters · AI video games · inventory management · recommendation engines
